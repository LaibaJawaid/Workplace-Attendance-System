🆘 Employee Help & Support Center

📸 Attendance Marking

  • To mark your Check-In or Check-Out, click on the **“Mark Attendance”** link in the side menu.
  • Ensure your face is **well-lit** and positioned correctly within the camera frame.
  • The system automatically determines if you are Checking In (first scan) or Checking Out (second scan).
  • If face is not recognized, try a different angle or lighting, or contact HR Helpdesk (ext. 102).

⚙️ Technical Support

  • If you see a blank screen, ensure your browser has camera access permissions.
  • For network or application errors, contact IT Support at support@workplace.com.
  • System is optimized for Chrome and Firefox browsers.
